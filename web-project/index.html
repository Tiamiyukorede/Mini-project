Make a mark with Custom Tattly!

We love partnering with people, brands, and organizations for Custom Tattly. From the White House to Christie's auction house, SoulCycle to Governor’s Ball, weddings and kids’ birthday parties, we will help you create fun memories by providing mementos for a special event, cause, or brand.

Need some inspiration? Visit custom.tattly.com to see some examples of recent Custom Tattly designs!

Do you have your own design ready to go for a Custom Tattly? Send an email to custom@tattly.com and we'll help you get started! (Order minimum of 1000 per design).

Tattly are:

  • Printed with vegetable-based ink
  • Made in the USA
  • Compliant with FDA regulations 
  • Safe for skin and fun for all ages!
